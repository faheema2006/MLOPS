Problem Statement:
The project focuses on developing an AI-powered career preparation and interview assistance platform called SkillSync to improve student employability, interview confidence, and placement readiness. Many students attend interviews without knowing whether their resume matches industry expectations or what technical and communication skills they need to improve for specific job roles. Traditional recruitment platforms mainly focus on filtering candidates rather than guiding students toward self-improvement and career development. The dataset used for the project contains columns such as Resume, Category, Skills, Experience, Education, Certifications, Projects, Job_Role, Required_Skills, Resume_Score, Skill_Gap, Interview_Questions, Candidate_Performance, Communication_Score, Technical_Score, and Recommendation_Output.
